THORNS & SHADOWS: The First Ember

Story

Pyris faces the decisive day: the Sylvan Trial Rite. To become a full-fledged Magic Guardian, he must venture deep into the most dangerous section of the forest and defeat the legendary Giant Wolf.

Only a few hours ago, Pyris learned the theory of fire magic. Now he must prove it in practice.

The forest is full of wild creatures, but by collecting primeval mana crystals, Pyris can channel the necessary heat into his staff to unleash his newly learned flames.

At the end of the path, the Giant Wolf awaits − lured by the howling of the beast, Pyris' true trial begins.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move Pyris through the forest. Collect mana crystals to recharge yout mana and use fire magic. Avoid or defeat enemies. Reach the end of the path to face the giant wolf and complete your trial.
